Description:
************

Something missing in your rFactor?
Something fast? Something loud?

You waited long, but finally CTDP brings you the real Formula One-experience to your rFactor. Now you can get the complete season 2005 with all cars, all polished, cleaned and prepared for your race. And that won't be a light one, but instead you get physics, which will demand all your cunning to succeed. Who said Formula One was easy, has never tried ... the real thing. 

I wish it was real!


Features:
*********

- 2005 Models and textures
- All models high details and textures high resolution
- All special liveries through the season
- 2005 helmets and driver
- 2005 Physics and Performance
- 2005 Rules (ie no tyre change, parc ferme)
- Non-linear tyregrip and tyre damages through debris
- 3 kinds of tyres for each of 2 manifacturer
- New tyres and rims with motion blur
- New Sounds by Fonsecker
- 10 Engine programms
- realistic wear of motor and brake 
- Updated Menu with Music by Michael Renk
- Updated Head Up Display
- Full DX7, DX8 and DX9- Support
- Supports rFactor-Skinning-Feature


Installation:
*************

1. To install the CTDP F1-2005, run the installation and install it into your rfactor-directory.
2. Select F1-2005 in your Mods-Menu in rFactor. Done, ready to go, simple as pie.
This mod is built for rFactor Version 1.1.3* or higher. Problems may occur with older versions of rFactor.

Everybody who doesn't want to earn all the cars, use this cheat to get around it.
Type ISI_BABYFACTORY in the chat-window of a testday and you can choose all cars.
