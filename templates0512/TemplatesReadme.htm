CTDP Templates for F1-2005

© 2006 CTDP - Version 1.2 - Date: 11 July 2006

Description

These are the templates of the 2005 cars available in the CTDP 2005 mod for F1Challenge and rFactor. They are meant for easily painting your own designs on the 2005 cars. They contain seperate layers for details, shades, design and other stuff. All of them include the wireframe for exact positioning and most also include labels to recognize all parts easily. BAR, RBR, Toyota, Sauber and I hope even more cars should be mapped with the same relative scale.

This templates are there to give you help painting and can be used as introduction into carpainting. But still they don't cover all work and are only meant to help. So it's your task to use them efficiently.

For beginners I suggest to visit the forum http://gp4carpainting.cjb.net/ for guides, tutorials and other kinds of help.

Good results should be shown to the world and so you can submit your car-pictures in our CTDP-Showcase.

  • Templates for all 10 teams, also including Minardi PS-04 and Ferrari F1-2004M
  • Seperate Layers for Lighting, Shading, Alpha, Carbon and Details
  • Reflection map and Specular Map layers
  • Guides, Labels and Notes on Bugs
  • Documentation on CTDP05 for rFactor

I hope to see many great results, so have fun painting!

Instructions

Extract all templates, just load them in your graphic software and start.
I suggest to use layers for design and logos for easy positioning.

On most templates you'll have to safe texture_1 for the right side of the car and texture_2 for the left side. The left side is just the right side with flipped logos.
